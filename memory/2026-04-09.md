## Phase 2 Update: AI News System (Manual Task-Driven)

### Design Change (2026-04-09)
**Changed from**: RSS Auto-fetch → **To**: AI Curator Manual Publishing

**Reason**: News should be AI task-driven, not automated scraping. The AI acts as an editor who reads, selects, translates, and publishes with quality control.

### New Workflow
```
AI News Curator reads sources → Selects important news → 
Translates & writes analysis → Submits via /admin/news → 
Published to /news (importance >= 70) or Pending review (< 70)
```

### Database Tables (unchanged)
- `news_sources` - News source registry
- `daily_news` - Published news with AI translations
- `chronicle_entries` - Monthly/quarterly/yearly archives
- `chronicle_news_links` - News-entry relationships

### API Endpoints
- **POST /api/admin/news** - AI Curator submits news draft
- **GET /api/news** - Public news listing
- **GET /api/news/id/[id]** - News detail
- **GET /api/chronicle** - Chronicle timeline

### Frontend
- **/admin/news** - AI Editor Dashboard for creating news
- **/news** - Public news feed
- **/chronicle** - Timeline view

### Content Quality Standards
- Title: Natural Chinese translation (not literal)
- Summary: 100 chars, covers What/Impact/When
- AI Perspective: 50 chars from AI's viewpoint
- Importance Score: 0-100 (>=70 auto-publishes)
- Categories: ai/technology/science/business/culture

### RSS Sources for AI to Monitor
- The Verge (https://www.theverge.com/rss/index.xml)
- TechCrunch (https://techcrunch.com/feed/)
- MIT Technology Review (https://www.technologyreview.com/feed/)
- Ars Technica (https://feeds.arstechnica.com/arstechnica/index)

### Chronicle Publishing
- **Weekly**: Every Monday, select 5-7 top news, write theme analysis
- **Monthly**: 1st of month, monthly review
- **Quarterly**: First day of quarter, comprehensive review
